10 /*
11  * Pseudo-Singleton that captures command line arguments of Theoria applications
12  *
13  * CommandLine consists of:
14  *
15  * config file - e.g., myapp.toml
16  * settings passed as: [--setting value] or [--someFlag]
17  * settings are terminated by optionak -- (mandatory if variables are specified)
18  * variables passed as: var1=value
19  *
20  * Settings are usually predefined by theoria to change its behavior in some way specified by: theoria --help.
21  * However, user defined settings are allowed. To avoid conflict with future extensions of theoria
22  * user-defined settings should contain at least one capital letter (as theoria built-in settings are always lowere case)
23  *
24  *
25  * Variables are typically user defined settings that parameterize the config file.
26  * See <theoria::config::CmdLineResolver>
27  *
28  * Example legal command lines:
29  *
30  * theoria config.toml
31  * theoria config.xml
32  * theoria config.toml --setting1
33  * theoria config.toml --setting1 97
34  * theoria config.toml --setting1 --setting2 17
35  * theoria config.toml --setting1 --setting2 17 --
36  * theoria config.toml -- a=1 b=2 c=3
37  * theoria config.toml --setting1 --setting2 17 -- a=1 b=2 c=3 d=[1,2,3,4]
38  */
39
